THEJOMAYA 2k19

Technical fest “THEJOMAYA 2k19” of Thejus Engineering College was inaugurated by Shri. Chandra Bose. J (Managing Director of SILK, SIFL & MIL) on 5th March 2019. The welcome address was given by Principal Dr. K. Vijayakumar. Mr. K. Kunhikrishnan, (Director, Thejus Group of Institutions) and Mr. V. S. Narayanan (PTA President) felicitated the function. The vote of thanks was proposed by Dr. Shajil N (HOD, Civil Engineering).
A bike stunt show and a bike mud race were conducted as promo activities as part of “THEJOMAYA 2k19”. Technical and fun events were organized by various departments of the college. A project expo and paper presentation contest were also conducted on that day. Around 500 students participated in various events, and the winners were awarded prize money. An intercollegiate dance and band competition was also held as a general event.
