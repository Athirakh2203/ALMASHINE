TECTALGIA

TECTALGIA is a global online arts and literary festival organized by the alumni of Government Engineering College (GEC) Thrissur:
TECTALGIA-I: The first TECTALGIA was conducted in March 2021 in two parts.
TECTALGIA-2: TECTALGIA-2 was a global online festival of talent show.
The GECT Global Alumni presented TECTALGIA-2 FB and YouTube Live on January 1, 2022.
GEC Thrissur is a prestigious technical education institution in south India. It was established in 1957 and is affiliated to the University of Calicut. The college offers undergraduate programs in Civil, Mechanical, and Electrical engineering.
