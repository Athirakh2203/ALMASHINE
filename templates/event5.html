DHANAK

The annual two-day cultural extravaganza – Dhanak has a unique flavor and style that makes it a much-expected event on RCET Campus. It’s a confluence of ideas, a perfect blend of the arts, the skills, and the passion to perform. Dance, light music, classical music, fashion show, literary debates, craft and many other events are organized, and leading celebrities are invited to judge the events. There’s competition and a spirit of camaraderie too, as students from various streams come together to participate, compete and win prizes. The Arts fest of RCET “DHANAK 2K18” was celebrated on 5, 6 March 2018 in the campus. The students showcased their creativity and innovations to a great extent. There were five stages inside the campus set at the Courtyard, Volleyball Court, parking lot, main seminar hall, and Room No.410. All the stages were active during the two days.
